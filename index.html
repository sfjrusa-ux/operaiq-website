Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know.

What types of businesses does OperaIQ work for?+
OperaIQ is designed for any service-based business with up to 25 employees — including trades (plumbers, electricians, cleaners), health & wellness (therapists, personal trainers, salons), professional services (accountants, consultants, lawyers), and more. If you take appointments, answer calls, and send invoices — OperaIQ is built for you.
How long does it take to get set up?+
Most clients are fully live within 48 hours of completing our onboarding questionnaire. We handle all the configuration, integrations, and testing on our end. You'll receive a 30-minute orientation call once everything is ready.
Do I need any technical skills or software?+
None at all. OperaIQ is a fully managed service. You access your dashboard through any web browser or our mobile app, and we handle all the technical infrastructure behind the scenes.
Does the AI phone answering really sound natural?+
Yes. We use best-in-class conversational AI that handles natural interruptions, follow-up questions, and complex booking scenarios. Most callers don't know they're speaking with an AI. You can listen to sample calls during your onboarding call.
Can I use my existing phone number and email?+
Yes. We can port your existing business phone number to the OperaIQ system, or set up call forwarding so there's no disruption to your business. For email, we connect to your existing Gmail or Outlook inbox.
What happens if I want to cancel?+
Monthly subscribers can cancel at any time with no penalty — you keep access until the end of your billing period. Annual subscribers can cancel but the year's subscription is non-refundable. All your data is exportable at any time.
